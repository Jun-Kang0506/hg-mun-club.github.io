What is MUN?

Model United Nations is an educational simulation where students can learn about diplomacy, international relations, and the United Nations. Participants role-play as delegates to the UN and simulate UN committees.

About Hiroo Gakuen MUN Club

Our club is dedicated to fostering an understanding of global affairs and developing essential skills like public speaking, negotiation, and critical thinking. We participate in various conferences and host internal training sessions.

Our Mission: To empower students to become informed global citizens and effective communicators through engaging MUN experiences.
